About VIT Chennai

The Chennai Campus of VIT University is a globally engaged, competitive research enriched campus, strategically located in a city that is one of the biggest automotive and it hubs in India. Its motto is to respond by rearing students to major industrial, social, economic and environmental demands and challenges of cosmopolitan cities in India and the world in general. This eight years old campus offers undergraduate, post graduate and research programmes and draws smart students for every state in India, through a highly competitive examination.

Rules And Regulations

Oral presentation (Individual)

  • > Topic of your choice
Requirements of the event
  • > PowerPoint/black board based oral Presentation of the topic for 7 minutes.
  • > Evaluation is based on Presentation of the topic Answering the questions posed by the judges.

Devil's Adovcate (Team of 2 Students)

  • > Topic will be given on the spot with nominal preparation time (3 minutes).
  • > Evaluation is based on Debating skills for/against the topic.

Working Model (Team of 4 Students)

  • > Topic of your choice
Requirements of the event
  • > Pre-designed working model.
  • > Model should explain the principle/concept of the topic displayed using figures/images/text.
  • > Duration of display of the working model to public - 2 hours Student is required to explain the topic using the working model as a tool.
  • >Evaluation is based on principle of the working model.
  • > Explanation of the concept by the student.
  • > Answering the questions raised by the judges.

Quiz (Team of 3 Students)

  • > Teams will be selected by written screening test.
  • > Quiz will be restricted to Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology and Technology.

Creativity in Science (Team of 2 Students)

  • > Any new concept based innovation in Science and Technology.

Buzz (Team of 2 Students)

  • > Any scientific theme can be expressed through clay or any material.
  • > Participants should bring the clay/ materials and colors required.
  • > Evaluation is based on Design pattern aesthetics Scientific theme depicted.

Watch and Reply (Individual)

  • > Visual round/clue round etc.

Dubsmath (Team of 4 members)

  • > Dumb charades – Questions from Mathematics, Science and Technology.

Rapid Fire round (Individual)

  • > To be answered within 5 seconds.
  • > Questions based on Science, Engineering and Technology.

e-Games (Individual)

  • > Name of the e-Games will be decided on the spot.
  • > Prelims may be conducted.
  • > Levels may be introduced for final decision.

Instructions to students

  • Students are requested to bring School ID card and register for the events on 24/2/2018 @ 09.00 am in Academic Block I entrance.
